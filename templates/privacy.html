Privacy Policy

We value transparency, security, and responsible data handling.

At Bitstack, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our university community. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data to empower your journey from learning to professional growth.

Why we use this information

Your data is utilized strictly to provide a seamless experience that balances the needs of Seekers and Owners within our ecosystem.

  • check_circle Account Management: Personalizing your dashboard and communication flow.
  • check_circle Precise Matching: Connecting Learners with Mentors and Freelancers with Clients based on skill symmetry.
  • check_circle Security & Safety: Proactive monitoring to prevent fraud and maintain platform standards.

Messaging & Platform Records

For the purpose of effective coordination and dispute resolution, Bitstack stores communications conducted through our internal messaging system. These records ensure accountability and provide a verifiable trail for collaborative projects, ensuring that all parties remain protected throughout the professional engagement.

Payments & BTS System

Transactions on Bitstack are handled through secure, industry-standard payment providers. We do not store sensitive credit card information on our servers.

Internal BTS Utility

The BTS internal utility system is a non-speculative rewards and coordination mechanism. It is used exclusively within Bitstack for internal tracking and is redeemable in TND only for verified users who meet our platform's professional criteria.
