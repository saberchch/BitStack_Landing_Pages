Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Bitstack is designed to protect users, institutions, mentors, freelancers, and clients through transparent data practices and secure platform workflows.

1

Introduction

Bitstack is a digital ecosystem connecting learners, mentors, freelancers, clients, institutes, and resource contributors. This Privacy Policy explains how Bitstack collects, uses, stores, and protects user information when users access the platform, create accounts, book services, publish resources, complete projects, or use BTS Credits.

2

Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide, improve, and personalize our services.

  • • Name, email address, phone number, and account credentials
  • • User role: learner, mentor, freelancer, client, institute, or contributor
  • • Profile details, skills, education, experience, and verification documents
  • • Bookings, mentoring sessions, project activity, uploaded resources, and BTS Credits transactions
  • • Messages, support requests, feedback, and dispute-related information
  • • Device, browser, IP address, and platform usage analytics
3

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to operate and improve the platform, verify users, match learners with mentors, connect clients with freelancers, manage projects, process BTS Credits activity, prevent misuse, communicate updates, and provide support.

4

BTS Credits Data

BTS Credits are internal platform credits used within the Bitstack ecosystem. We may record BTS-related activity such as purchases, rewards, refunds, project payments, mentoring payments, balance updates, and transaction history. BTS Credits are not treated as public cryptocurrency or external financial assets.

5

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary for platform operation, legal compliance, payment processing, user verification, dispute resolution, or service delivery between users.

6

User Content and Resources

Users may upload learning resources, project files, profile information, or portfolio items. Bitstack may display selected public content according to the user's role, permissions, and platform settings.

7

Data Protection

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

8

User Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of your personal information, subject to applicable laws, platform obligations, and security requirements.

9

Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to improve navigation, understand platform usage, enhance user experience, and monitor performance.

10

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any major updates will be communicated through the platform or published on this page.

11

Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, you may contact us through our Contact Page or the Help Center.
