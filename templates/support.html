Frequently Asked

How long do transfers take?

Standard transfers process within 1-2 business days. Premium tier accounts have access to instant liquidity options subject to standard risk checks.

How do I verify my identity?

Navigate to Settings > Profile Verification. You will need a government-issued ID and a proof of address document dated within the last 90 days.

Are there withdrawal limits?

Base tier accounts have a daily withdrawal limit of $10,000. This limit scales based on account verification level and historical volume.
