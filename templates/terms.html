Payments & BTS System

Bitstack utilizes an internal utility unit called BTS to facilitate seamless transactions within the platform ecosystem. BTS is designed for functional utility and internal accounting.

  • Non-Speculative: BTS has a fixed internal value and is not a tradable cryptocurrency or investment vehicle.
  • Redemption: BTS can only be redeemed for Tunisian Dinars (TND) by authorized users who have passed mandatory identity verification.
  • Authorized Use: BTS must be used exclusively for services provided on Bitstack, such as paying for mentorship or freelance deliverables.
