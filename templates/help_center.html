Common Questions

?
How do I join Bitstacks?

Create an account, choose your role (Mentor, Freelancer, or Institute), and complete your profile to start exploring opportunities.

?
How do I book a mentor?

Browse our directory of verified mentors, select a session time, and confirm your booking using your BTS Credits balance.

?
How do BTS Credits work?

BTS Credits are our internal platform currency used for booking services, earning rewards, and processing payouts.

?
How do freelancers get paid?

Freelancers receive BTS Credits automatically after a client approves a project milestone or completion.

?
How do I contact support?

You can reach out to our team using the contact form on this page or by clicking the support button at the bottom.
